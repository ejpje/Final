The Viking Age

During the Viking Age (c.700-1100 CE), Vikings from modern-day Norway, Sweden, and Denmark raided throughout Europe, northern Africa, and western Asia, establishing settlements and trade routes that would ultimately outlive them.

Use the slider to switch between the centuries, or use the layer menu to change the background map or which Viking group is shown.
